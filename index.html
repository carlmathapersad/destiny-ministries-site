Meet Our Pastor

DR Shaun Mannie

Dr Shaun Mannie is the Senior Pastor and visionary leader of Destiny Ministries, based in Johannesburg, South Africa. Serving faithfully alongside his beloved wife, Mrs Rani Mannie, Dr Shaun is a devoted shepherd holding a Doctoral degree in theology. Guided by a deep love for God's people, his life’s mission is to nurture believers and help them discover the beautiful, God-given purpose waiting within them. Dr Shaun teaches the Word of God with profound spiritual wisdom and grace. His ministry does not just focus on traditional church routines; instead, he seeks to cultivate authentic character, pure hearts, and a deep, intimate relationship with the Lord.
